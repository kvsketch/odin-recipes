Lasagna

Master Recipe

This recipe will knock you out with flavor!

Ingredients

  • 1(16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • a copy of The Matrix on DVD
  • 1 pound of MEAT
  • 3/4 cup chopped green bell pepper
  • 3/4 cup chopped onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 jars pasta sauce
  • 4 pounds of mozzarella cheese
  • egg
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Begin by putting in all your ingredients(refer above) in a blueray player
  2. jam your pristine DVD copy of The Matrix into the blueray player
  3. your lasagna should be ready to eat!
