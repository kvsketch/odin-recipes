Naan

Naan of ya'Business!

A naan recipe that remains in mystery!

Ingredients

  • 1 hardhat
  • 1 to 2 flashlight(s)
  • 2 hiking boots

Steps

  1. begin by exiting your home
  2. walk until you find an old man with a cloak on
  3. talk to old man until he gives you the quest Pieces of the Past
  4. complete the old man's quest giving you access to the naan cave
  5. enter the naan cave
  6. you will find your naan waiting for you
